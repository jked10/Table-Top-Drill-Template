How it works

From procedure to scored drill in four steps

No scenario writing. No spreadsheets. Upload once and DrillFrame builds the exercise around your own plan.

01

Upload your procedure

Drop in your emergency plan, security plan, or SOP — PDF or Word. It stays in your browser.

02

Pick the scenario

DrillFrame reads the document and offers the specific emergencies it covers. Choose one and set the difficulty.

03

Run the drill

Walk the team through decision injects on a shared screen, or send a join link so everyone answers on their phone.

04

Score & sign off

Get per-person and per-role scores, a team result, and an audit-ready report to export as PDF.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need any software installed?+

No. DrillFrame runs in any modern browser — on a laptop for facilitator mode, or on participants' phones for live mode. Nothing to install.

Where do my uploaded plans go?+

They're read entirely inside your browser to build the scenario — the document itself is never uploaded to a server. Your reusable scenario libraries are stored locally per organisation.

What's this about an AI key?+

DrillFrame uses AI to turn your procedure into scenarios. On Pro you connect a low-cost AI key in about two minutes (we walk you through it) — a typical drill costs a fraction of a cent to generate. We're rolling out a fully-managed option where you won't need a key at all.

Can I use it for any kind of drill?+

Yes — emergency response, fire and evacuation, security (ISPS and others), safety, and standard operating procedures. If it's a written plan, you can rehearse it.

What if I'd rather you just ran it for us?+

That's the Facilitated Drill option. We build the scenario from your plan and run the whole exercise for your team, then hand over a signed report. $500 per session locally in the US; overseas quoted on request.
